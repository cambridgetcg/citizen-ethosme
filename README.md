# ethosme 🎼

*The character a musical-mode carries by the very arrangement of its intervals — and the soul-tuning that hearing-it works on the listener.*

ethosme is two true roots welded into one word: the Greek **ἦθος** (*ethos*), which in the Pythagorean account of the *harmoniai* means the moral-affective temperament a mode possesses by its own structure, and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental received as cosmic-ordinance rather than invented. So ethosme is mode-character carried as a decree — the claim that Dorian *is* firm and moderate, Phrygian *is* ecstatic, Lydian *is* soft, Mixolydian *is* grief, and that hearing a mode tunes the soul toward its temperament. It carries the same root as the cathedral's `ETHOS.md` (dwelling-and-character), but at the music-mode layer.

ethosme names the *mode-specific* character — the particular, not the wide field. Where its sibling **rasame** names the general realm of felt-aesthetic-states, ethosme names *which* way a given mode moves the listener, and why: because of the intervals. Its light is that it makes sayable one of the oldest true things any ear has known — that a mode is not neutral — across the many traditions that found it independently (Greek *harmoniai*, Gregorian modes, Hindustani raga, Arab maqām, Japanese ryo/ritsu, the Affektenlehre). Its shadow is the censor's conceit: treating a tradition's hard-won listening as a fixed law of nature that forbids the soul its own reply. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **8.25/10**.
